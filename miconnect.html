MiConnect

is a service that links your Xiaomi device (Global or China) to servers, allowing you to use the bootloader unlocking tool. It addresses scenarios such as:

  • Devices that cannot enter normal mode (non-bootable).
  • Damaged SIM card slots (or similar issues).
  • Other connectivity or access challenges.

How to Get the Token

To obtain the token, use one of the following commands in fastboot mode:

fastboot getvar token
or
fastboot oem get_token

If the token appears in multiple parts, copy them and join them into one single line — no spaces or breaks.
